What's new with iOS 18

Posted on 2024-09-19 Edited on 2024-09-18 Disqus:


There are so many new features on iOS 18. Without upgrading iPhone, we can use them for just upgrading it into iOS 18.

Apple Intelligence

By Apple Intelligence, your iPhone will become your own secretary. You can ask for next meeting, organize daily tasks and even searching for specific photos.

iOS 18.1 with NFC enabled

On iOS 18.1, developers can use new APIs that support secure contactless transactions using NFC. There are so many features can be done, like in-store payments, car keys, student ID, home keys, event tickets, etc.

Genmoji

Generate your own emoji. genmoji is featured by AI. Your emoji will become the only one and owned by you.

Customization

Changing color on Home screen and Lock screen are now possible. Without changing the iOS look, we can change the color we can.

Camera Control

Okay, I really want this feature. But this feature only available on iPhone 16. Camera Control will give you more control of your camera with new button on the right side. With this button, we can easily open the camera and zoom the object by dragging on it (yeah, it has touch feature).

Photos

With new photos app, you can organize your photos easily, from faces to places.

Game Mode

Game mode will bring more performance to your gaming experience by reducing the background processes and freeing some resources.

New Control Center

It has multiple screens, groups of controls, music controller, connectivity controllers, etc.

Control with macOS Sequoia

If your macOS upgrade to Sequoia, you can easily remote your iPhone with your macOS easily.
