My Flutter Project

Posted on 2024-07-12 Edited on 2024-09-18 Disqus:

Hi everyone, I’ve just completed a Flutter app called BabyDiary. You can check out my project on GitHub.

After developing with Flutter, I understand why it is so popular:

  • Backed by Google: Flutter benefits from Google’s robust support and ongoing development.
  • Vibrant Community: There’s a large and active community, making it easier to find resources and support.
  • Extensive Plugins: A wide variety of plugins simplify integration and enhance functionality.
  • Ease of Use: The framework is intuitive, making it easy to use even for those new to app development.
  • Rapid Development: Flutter allows for fast prototyping and development, which is especially beneficial for app developers.

While I enjoyed on Flutter, I have a few reservations:

  • Limited Adoption by Google Apps: Unlike Facebook’s use of React Native, Google’s own apps are not widely developed with Flutter.
  • Non-Native Rendering: Flutter uses the Skia rendering engine, meaning the UI isn’t natively rendered.
  • Manual Animations: Some default animations (e.g., fade in/out) that are built into iOS’s native UI need to be manually implemented in Flutter.

Feel free to check out BabyDiary, and I’d love to hear your thoughts or experiences with Flutter!
